A Letter From Roy & Ami

When we first founded The LR Group in 1985, our goal was to run a successful business. Over the years we realized that we had chanced upon the opportunity to not only do business, but also profoundly impact the lives of the people in the countries where our projects were implemented. We understood that while fulfilling our dreams we were able to make a difference in the world.

We share a deep belief in the power of our business model for providing sustainable solutions that create economic value for society by addressing its needs and challenges, ensuring positive impact and growth.

This is what we have been doing for the past 3 decades. We have expanded our fields of expertise and have implemented hundreds of sustainable projects not only in Africa but also in Europe, Oceania and South America in the fields of Agriculture, Water, Infrastructure, Healthcare, Energy, Communications, Education and training. With our employees irrepressible determination, vision and integrity, we have established a track record in translating big ideas into long term solutions.

As we look back on the last 30 years, we are proud of what we have achieved. Looking ahead, together with our dedicated and passionate team, we are humbled and excited to continue to provide thousands, if not hundreds of thousands of lives with a healthier and happier future.

Roy Ben-Yami & Ami Lustig
Co-founders.
