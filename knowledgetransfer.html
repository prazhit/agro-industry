Pandey Agro's Knowledge Transfer Program

Pandey Agro's Training & Knowledge Transfer – The essential component that ensures the long term success of each and every project.

Pandey Agro Training & Knowledge Transfer – The essential component that ensures the long term success of each and every project.LR Group operates field tours, workshops, coaching, professional counseling, and a wide variety of training programs that accomplish our ultimate goal of knowledge transfer. By training the next generation of professionals, we are able to inculcate best-practices, install the leading technologies, and ensure that each project can rise above its individual challenges to achieve sustainability.

Our experience shows that an extensive educational process, combined with the introduction of new technologies, encourages personal and regional development, empowering our local stakeholders.

Our Training & Knowledge Transfer GOALS
INCLUDE

Developing training frameworks to ensure social impact and sustainability
Demonstrating improved practices and presentinginnovative technologieswhich will improve productivity and livelihoods
Expansion of professional and technical knowledge
Empowering employees and local stakeholders to enhance human resource capabilities.
Integration of traditional and modern practices
Establishing a community of local professionals who will continue to implement theacquired knowledge
