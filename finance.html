Finance Facilitation

“In each and every project that is set in motion, the experience & know-how of the finance facilitation division plays a key role in making our vision become a reality.”

Shuki Raz, Director Project & Structure Finance

The LR Group providesone-stop-shop service for development projects around the world, including engineering financing packages. LR Group’s experienced financial team is trusted by financial institutions, including leading international banks and insurance institutions,and is able to provide our clients with the necessary tools tobring the projects to life.

Unique Financing and Facilitation Solutions

Throughout the last several decades, our experts have successfully brought together the many parties involved in financing a wide range of projects and have navigated the negotiations for complex financial solutions.

We conduct full bankability assessments of potential ventures and offer financing structures of all kinds, providing financial backing and optimized funding options.

Financing Structures for Diverse Enterprises

Our professional team works with each client to customize the ideal financing structure for each project. These all-inclusive financial solutions are tailor-made and optimize all project elements. They include everything from BOT, BOO, and PPP models with stakeholder participation, Export finance, Trade finance, andProject &Structure finance.
