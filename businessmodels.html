Our Business Models

EPC

Engineering, Procurement, and Construction – In this full turnkey model LR Group takes charge of design & planning, procurement of all necessary materials, and construction of the project, strictly adhering to the agreed budget, schedule, and high quality standards (performance).

EPCM

Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Management – LR Group provides management services in addition to all of the services of the EPC model.

BOO

Build, Own,and Operate – This model puts LR Group in charge of the development, finance, engineering, procurement, construction, andongoing operation of the project.BOO projects may be developed either by LR Group exclusively or in cooperation with other partners.

BOOT

Build, Own, Operate, Transfer – Although similar to the BOO model, when using this model LR Group transfers the facility to the public administration at the end of the concession agreement.

PPP

Public-Private Partnership – In this model LR Group enters into a partnership with the local government to provide innovative technology and expertise, improveservice, and facilitate economic growth.
