SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Wherever LR is involved we like to do everything we can to change people’s reality for the better. We achieve this through our projects but we don’t stop there. We try to look deeper into the society to contribute where we are needed most.

Some of the channels for this contribution include urgent medical care and providing shelter and care for children in need. Through these causes, in each community we are able to bring hope to the people who need it most. The organizations we work with truly change people’s everyday existence, enabling them to build meaningful, productive futures and giving us another reason to enjoy our work.

Save A Child’s Heart

Save A Child’s Heart (SACH) is one of the largest undertakings in the world, providing urgently needed pediatric heart surgery and follow-up care for indigent children from developing countries. Based in Israel, Save a Child’s Heart mission is to improve the quality of pediatric cardiac care for children from countries where the heart surgery they need is unobtainable. Save a Child’s Heart also offers a comprehensive training program within Israel for doctors and nurses from developing countries, and leads surgical and teaching missions to our partner countries.We are very pleased to contribute to such an important mission. To this day LR Group has helped dozens of children from around the world to receive medical treatment in Israel.

Eye From Zion

A voluntary organization from Israel carrying out medical humanitarian missions throughout the world. The organization provides free medical assistance to whoever needs it but cannot afford it by sending medical teams and equipment anywhere and also provides instruction and guidance to medical teams in developing countries.LR is proud to be part of this mission and to have provided support for hundreds of ophthalmological exams and 102 eyes surgeries in DRC.

Kinkole Orphanage

Kinkole Orphanage is located just 5km from LR Group operations at Nouveau DAIPN Farm in DRC. The orphanage provides a home and foster care to sixty children ranging from 7 to 18 years of age. LR Group is proud to be part of the assistance given to these children through the donation of food and furniture as well as the promotion of social and cultural activities
